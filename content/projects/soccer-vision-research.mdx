# Overview
Research framework for multi-model soccer video analysis. Combines RF-DETR for detection, SAM2 for segmentation with temporal tracking, and SigLIP for zero-shot player identification. Modular architecture with YAML-driven config presets, intelligent result fusion, and 5 interactive demo applications.
