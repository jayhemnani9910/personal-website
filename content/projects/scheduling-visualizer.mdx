# Overview
Interactive CPU scheduling algorithm visualizer implementing 9 algorithms (FCFS, SJF, SRTF, LJF, LRTF, Priority NP/P, Round Robin, HRRN) with Gantt charts, comparative analysis, and configurable parameters. The scheduling concepts were applied to vaccine distribution in a published IEEE paper at AIMV 2021.
