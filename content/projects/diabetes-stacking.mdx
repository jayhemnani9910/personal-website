# Overview
Stacking ensemble classifier for early diabetes prediction, published at the 2021 International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Machine Vision (AIMV). Combines 6 base classifiers with cross-validated hyperparameter tuning and a Logistic Regression meta-learner on the PIMA Indians Diabetes Dataset.
