# Overview
Physics-informed deep learning framework for protein stability and enzyme kinetics prediction. Uses a novel tri-modal architecture combining ProtT5 sequence embeddings, GATv2 graph attention with Vibrational Density of States from Normal Mode Analysis, and ChemBERTa chemical encodings. Two-phase design: QDD framework (Phase 1-2) for general molecular property prediction and VibroPredict (Phase 3) for enzyme catalytic turnover prediction.
