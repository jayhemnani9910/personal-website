## Template Variables
- `{{COMPANY}}` — Company name
- `{{ROLE}}` — Role title
- `{{OPENING_HOOK}}` — 1-2 sentences connecting background to role (pull from resume.ts)
- `{{EXPERIENCE_MATCH}}` — 2-3 paragraphs matching experience to job requirements
- `{{SKILLS_MATCH}}` — Bullet list of matching skills with evidence
- `{{CLOSER}}` — Availability + enthusiasm + differentiator (WebMCP, IEEE pubs, etc.)
