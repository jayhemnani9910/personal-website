# Outreach & Application Templates

## 1. LinkedIn Connection Request (to hiring managers)

```
Hi [Name], I'm a full-stack developer (Python, React/Next.js, cloud) actively
looking for remote opportunities. I saw [Company] is hiring for [Role] and I'd
love to learn more. My portfolio: jayhemnani.me
```

## 2. Cold Email to Startups

```
Subject: Entry-level developer — remote, available immediately

Hi [Name],

I'm Jay, a full-stack developer looking for remote opportunities. I build with
Python, React/Next.js, and cloud infra (AWS/GCP). I'm particularly interested
in [Company] because [specific reason].

Portfolio: jayhemnani.me
GitHub: github.com/jayhemnani9910

I'd love to chat about how I can contribute. Available immediately.

Best,
Jay Hemnani
```

## 3. Cover Letter Template

```
Dear Hiring Team,

I'm writing to express my interest in the [Role] position at [Company].

As a full-stack developer with hands-on experience across Python, JavaScript
(React/Next.js), and cloud infrastructure, I bring [specific skill matching
job requirement]. My portfolio at jayhemnani.me showcases [relevant project]
which demonstrates [relevant skill].

What excites me about [Company] is [specific reason — product, mission, tech
stack]. I'm available immediately for remote work and eager to contribute to
[team/product].

I'd welcome the opportunity to discuss how my skills align with your needs.

Best regards,
Jay Hemnani
jayhemnani.me | github.com/jayhemnani9910
```

## 4. Follow-Up Email (5-7 days after applying)

```
Subject: Following up — [Role] application

Hi [Name],

I applied for the [Role] position on [date] and wanted to follow up. I'm very
interested in [Company] and believe my experience with [relevant tech] would
be a great fit.

Happy to share more details or jump on a quick call at your convenience.

Best,
Jay Hemnani
```

## 5. Thank You After Interview

```
Subject: Thank you — [Role] interview

Hi [Name],

Thank you for taking the time to speak with me about the [Role] position. I
enjoyed learning about [something specific from the conversation].

I'm even more excited about the opportunity after our discussion, particularly
[specific aspect]. I'm confident I can contribute meaningfully to [team/project].

Looking forward to hearing from you.

Best,
Jay Hemnani
```
